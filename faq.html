Frequently Asked Questions

We welcome all dogs, including those with special needs—blind, deaf, neurodivergent, mobility-impaired, and more.

We serve the Greater St. Louis area and offer mobile services across the metro region.

Do you offer consultations?

Yes! We offer free 15-minute phone consultations to help you decide what’s best for your dog.

Can I book recurring sessions?

Absolutely. We offer flexible scheduling for weekly, biweekly, or custom plans.
